The Game

As a part of this project, we have developed an educational and entertaining board game called "Navium", intended for all generations. The game promotes renewable energy sources and encourages the use of hydrogen ships. In the game, players can upgrade their ships, trade with others and build power plants. When you build four powerplants you are declared the Winner.

Creation of the Game

The game consists of 6 different material cards (60 all together) and 20 different action cards (50 all together). Figurines that represent hydrogen-powered ships and electrical powerplants on renewable and non-renewable energy sources are also included. The game board, which is made out of cardboard, also functions as the box for the game.

The Goals of the Game

We created "Navium" to help people understand the environmental impact of the use of fossil fuels on our beloved planet in a fun and original way. We want people to understand why and how they can do their part by purchasing this game.
